Travel-funding-tips

Information on how to fundraise and crowdfund your travel to volunteer.

Welcome!

Are you so inspired by progressive candidates running for seats in contested states that you’re going to travel there to help? That’s awesome! We salute you.
Saving the world can get expensive, what with the cost of gas, flights, hotels, and eating out. That’s why we created this little guide to crowdfunding for your travel expenses.
There are many people who want progressive candidates to win, but are unable to leave their homes (whether because of work or family obligations).
Let them sponsor your efforts! Follow these easy steps:

  1. Figure out how much money you’ll need and subtract how much you can afford to pay yourself. That should be your crowdfunding goal. Be sure to include these items:
    1. Gas money or cost of a flight/train/bus to get there
    2. Charges for hostels/hotels/AirBnBs you may stay in
    3. Some money for the eating out you’ll be doing
  2. Create your project page on GoFundMe or CrowdRise or FundRazr
    1. Use the amount calculated above
    2. Add a personal story about why this candidate’s win is important to you and how thankful you will be for people’s support.
    3. Add a picture of you, the candidate, or a particular cause
    4. Send donation requests to your friends, family, and friends of friends
    5. Post on social media (Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, Instagram) with a link to your page
    6. In all cases, talk about WHEN you need the money by
  3. Keep crowdfunding! People need many nudges to remember to donate
    1. Every few days, post on social media about your progress
    2. Once a week later send out emails to everyone about your progress and what’s left. Don’t forget to remind them of the importance of the cause.
  4. Go save the world! (Try to keep your costs down by using HillaryBnB or Couchsurfing)
    • Post photos to social media of you in action and encourage more donations to cover your efforts
