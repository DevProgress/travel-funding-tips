Travel for Progress Crowdfunding

Information on how to fundraise and crowdfund your travel to volunteer.

Are you so inspired by progressive candidates running for seats in contested states that you’re going to travel there to help? That’s awesome! We salute you.

Saving the world can get expensive, what with the cost of gas, flights, hotels, and eating out. Crowdfunding can help you raise the money you need to cover your costs.

There are many people who want progressive candidates to win, but are unable to leave their homes (whether because of work or family obligations).

Let them sponsor your efforts!

New to crowdfunding? Don’t worry! We’ve created this easy guide to get you started.

Set a Goal

Figure out how much money you’ll need and subtract how much you can afford to pay yourself. That should be your crowdfunding goal.

These are some common expenses for volunteers:

  • Travel: How do you plan to travel to the location where you’ll volunteer? Calculate how much you’ll need to spend on gas money or a plane, train, or bus ticket.
  • Lodging: Where do you plan to stay? Add up how much the hostels/hotels/AirBnBs will cost. Try to keep your costs down by using HillaryBnB or Couchsurfing.
  • Food: Figure out how much you will need to pay for three meals a day and other personal expenses (coffee, snacks, extra toothpaste/shampoo, etc.).

Create a Project Page

You can create your crowdfunding page on GoFundMe, CrowdRise, or FundRazr. When creating your page, follow these guidelines:

  • State your fundraising goal (calculated above)
  • Tell your story. Let supporters know why this candidate’s win matters to you and how thankful you will be for their support.
  • Add a photo. An eye-catching visual will help attract supporters. You can use a photo of you, the candidate, or an image that calls attention to a particular cause.
  • Be specific. Tell supporters exactly how the funds will be used—for transportation, lodging, food, etc.
  • Set a deadline: State clearly the date by which you will need the money.s

Spread the Word

Asking for donations can feel intimidating, but it is essential to meeting your fundraising goal.

  • Send personalized requests. Email family and friends asking for their support.
  • Post on social media. You can use Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, and Instagram to reach a wider circle of acquaintances. Be sure link to your crowdfunding page and ask friends to share your post.
  • Send reminders. People need many nudges to remember to donate. Every few days, post on social media about your progress, and send email updates once a week. Don’t forget to remind potential supporters of why your cause is important.
  • Thank your supporters. And be sure to tell them why their donations matter to you.

Go save the world!

Post photos to social media of you in action and encourage more donations to cover your efforts.
